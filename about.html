Origin

Engineered, not decorated.

I trained on high-performance diesel engines, where a tolerance of a fraction of a millimetre decides whether something runs or fails. That standard doesn't leave you.

Sirius Ascent applies it to the web. Every pixel, every interaction, every millisecond of load time — engineered to perform.

Why Sirius Ascent exists

Helping local businesses look as good as they actually are.

Most local businesses do great work and still look invisible or outdated online. Sirius Ascent exists to close that gap — building premium websites, managing Google Business Profiles consistently, and building trust with customers before they ever walk through the door.

Future goals

We're building toward a full-service studio for South African small businesses — premium websites, ongoing management and local SEO under one roof, with a portfolio of flagship projects that speak for themselves.
