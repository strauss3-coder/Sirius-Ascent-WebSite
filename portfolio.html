Case study

Supreme Auto — Google Business Profile.

Real client, real numbers. This is exactly what one month of active management did for an independent car dealership in Pretoria North.

The client

Supreme Auto, Pretoria North

Supreme Auto is an independent used-car dealership. Their Google Business Profile had sat untouched for months — no new photos, no posts, no replies to reviews. We visited the dealership in person and rebuilt it from the ground up: fresh photography, weekly posts, and active review responses.

Before / after

Drag to compare.

After
Before

Before: a handful of old photos, no recent posts, no activity for months — to a customer scrolling Google Maps, this reads as closed.

After: dozens of fresh photos and weekly updates — the same dealership now looks open, professional and worth visiting.

On location

We don't ask for photos. We go and take them.

Every image on Supreme Auto's profile was shot on-site by Sirius Ascent — not stock photography, not a phone snap from the owner.
