Case study · Website + Google Business Profile

Supreme Auto — the full package.

For Supreme Auto we built the complete dealership website and manage their Google Business Profile — the storefront and the visibility that leads customers to it.

The website

A dealership site that sells the whole lot.

Filterable inventory by make, model, price and body type, detailed vehicle pages with photo galleries and specs, a finance application and direct enquiry forms — a genuine online showroom.

Google Business Profile

Proof before promises.

Alongside the website, we rebuilt and actively manage Supreme Auto's Google Business Profile. It had sat untouched for months — no new photos, no posts, no replies to reviews. We visited the dealership in person and rebuilt it from the ground up: fresh photography, weekly posts and active review responses. Here's exactly what one month of active management did.
