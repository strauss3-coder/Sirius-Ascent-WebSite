## Mission log — α · Supreme Auto

_Charted, built, and risen. Now holding its own coordinates on the open web._

### Challenge

Supreme Auto did strong work on the ground and looked invisible online. No site
worth the name, an inconsistent Google presence, and no way for buyers to browse
stock before driving out.

### Research

We audited what local buyers actually search for, how competitor dealerships
ranked, and where the dealership's Google Business Profile was leaking attention.

### Design strategy

A search-first storefront: inventory legible at a glance, finance and enquiry one
tap away, every screen built mobile-first because that's where the buyers are.

### Development

Hand-built, lightweight front end — no bloated page builder. Semantic markup,
structured data for vehicles, and images optimised so the whole thing loads fast
on a phone signal.

### Performance

Sub-second loads, clean Core Web Vitals, and a Google Business Profile brought
back to life with weekly posts and on-site photography.

### Result

The site is **risen** — live on its own domain, sending real enquiries and
direction requests to the dealership every week.
